Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Brown sugar
  • White sugar
  • Eggs
  • Baking soda
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl
  3. Combine brown sugar, white sugar, and butter in a seperate bowl
  4. Slownly fold dry mixutre in sugar and butter mixture
  5. Fold eggs and mix
  6. Add vanilla extract
  7. Scoop dough onto baking sheet
  8. Bake until cookies are golden on edges
