Pasta Arrabiata

Ingredients

  • 1 box of dry pasta
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp of dried chili pepper
  • 1 small can of olives
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Boil pasta for 10 minutes
  2. Combine tomato sauce, chili pepper, salt, and pepper in a large sauce pan
  3. Cook sauce for 2 minutes
  4. Add cooked pasta to sauce and cook for 1 minute
  5. Add olives when ready to serve
